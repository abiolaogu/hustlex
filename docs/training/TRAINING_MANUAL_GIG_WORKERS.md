## Module 1: Setting Up Your Profile (20 min)

### 1.1 Why Your Profile Matters

Your profile is your first impression. Clients see it when reviewing proposals, and a complete profile gets **3x more job offers**.

### 1.2 Profile Completion Checklist

| Element | Impact | Status |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Profile Photo | Builds trust | [ ] |
| Full Name | Professional identity | [ ] |
| Bio/Summary | Shows expertise | [ ] |
| Skills | Matching to gigs | [ ] |
| Portfolio | Proves ability | [ ] |
| BVN Verification | Higher tier access | [ ] |

### 1.3 Writing Your Bio

**Template:**
```
I am a [profession] with [X years] of experience in [specialization].
I specialize in [specific skills] and have worked with [types of clients].
My goal is to [value you provide].
```

**Example:**
```
I am a graphic designer with 5 years of experience in brand identity design.
I specialize in logo design, social media graphics, and brand guidelines.
I have worked with startups, small businesses, and established companies.
My goal is to help businesses stand out with professional, memorable visuals.
```

### 1.4 Choosing Your Skills

**Tips:**
- Select 5-10 relevant skills
- Be specific (not just "design" but "logo design", "UI design")
- Only list skills you're confident in
- Update as you learn new skills

### 1.5 Exercise: Profile Setup

1. Open your HustleX app
2. Go to Profile → Edit Profile
3. Upload a professional photo
4. Write your bio using the template
5. Add at least 5 skills
6. Start BVN verification

## Module 2: Finding the Right Gigs (25 min)

### 2.1 Understanding Gig Categories

| Category | Types of Work |
|----------|---------------|
| Technology | Web/mobile development, IT support, data entry |
| Design | Graphic design, UI/UX, illustrations |
| Writing | Content writing, copywriting, translation |
| Marketing | Social media, SEO, advertising |
| Business | Virtual assistance, customer service, accounting |
| Lifestyle | Event planning, personal shopping, tutoring |

### 2.2 Using Filters Effectively

**Steps:**
1. Tap "Gigs" tab
2. Tap filter icon
3. Select:
   - Category matching your skills
   - Budget range you're comfortable with
   - Deadline you can meet
4. Apply filters

### 2.3 Reading Gig Descriptions

**What to Look For:**
- Clear requirements
- Realistic budget for the scope
- Reasonable deadline
- Client rating (if visible)

**Red Flags:**
- Vague requirements
- Too much work for budget
- Unrealistic deadline
- Requests for work before acceptance

### 2.4 Exercise: Gig Search

1. Open Gigs tab
2. Set filters for your skills
3. Browse 5 gigs
4. Identify 2 that match your abilities
5. Note why the others don't fit

## Module 3: Writing Winning Proposals (30 min)

### 3.1 Anatomy of a Great Proposal

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ HOOK (1-2 sentences)                            │
│ Grab attention, show you understand the need    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ EXPERIENCE (2-3 sentences)                      │
│ Relevant past work that proves you can deliver  │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ APPROACH (2-3 sentences)                        │
│ How you'll tackle this specific project         │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ CALL TO ACTION (1 sentence)                     │
│ Invite further discussion                       │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 3.2 Proposal Template

```
Hi [Client name if available],

[HOOK]
I noticed you need [specific thing from gig]. I've done exactly this for
[similar project/client] and would love to help you achieve [their goal].

[EXPERIENCE]
In my previous work, I [specific achievement relevant to this gig].
For example, I [concrete example with results if possible].

[APPROACH]
For your project, I would start by [first step], then [second step],
ensuring [quality/timeline commitment].

[CALL TO ACTION]
I'm available to start immediately. Would you like to discuss the details?

Best regards,
[Your name]
```

### 3.3 Proposal Examples

**Bad Proposal:**
```
Hi, I can do this job. I have experience. Please hire me.
```

**Good Proposal:**
```
Hi,

I saw you need a logo for your new restaurant. I specialize in
food and beverage branding and have designed logos for 15+ restaurants
in Lagos.

Last month, I created a full brand identity for a local café that helped
them increase customer recognition by 40% (according to their survey).

For your restaurant, I'd start by understanding your cuisine and target
customers, then create 3 initial concepts for you to choose from.
I guarantee 2 rounds of revisions within your timeline.

I'm excited about this project and available to start today.
Shall we discuss?

Best,
Chidi
```

### 3.4 Pricing Your Services

**Factors to Consider:**
- Scope of work
- Your experience level
- Time required
- Market rates for similar work
- Client's budget range

**Strategy:**
- Don't always go for the lowest price
- Price within the middle-to-upper range
- Justify your price with value

### 3.5 Exercise: Write a Proposal

1. Select one of the gigs you identified
2. Write a proposal using the template
3. Review against the criteria
4. Submit your proposal

## Module 4: Delivering Quality Work (25 min)

### 4.1 After Your Proposal is Accepted

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Review contract terms carefully
- [ ] Clarify any unclear requirements
- [ ] Confirm deadline
- [ ] Set milestones (for larger projects)
- [ ] Begin work immediately

### 4.2 Communication Best Practices

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Respond within 24 hours | Ghost the client |
| Ask clarifying questions | Assume you understand |
| Provide progress updates | Disappear until deadline |
| Be professional | Use slang excessively |
| Document agreements | Trust verbal promises |

### 4.3 Managing Your Time

**Tips:**
- Break work into daily tasks
- Start early (don't wait until deadline)
- Build in buffer time for revisions
- Communicate if you'll be delayed

### 4.4 Submitting Deliverables

**Steps:**
1. Complete your work
2. Review against requirements
3. Organize files clearly
4. Write a delivery message
5. Submit through the app
6. Respond promptly to feedback

**Delivery Message Template:**
```
Hi [Client],

I've completed [what you delivered]. Here's what's included:
- [Item 1]
- [Item 2]
- [Item 3]

[Any notes about the delivery]

Please review and let me know if you need any revisions.

Thank you!
```

### 4.5 Handling Revision Requests

**Approach:**
- Read feedback carefully
- Ask questions if unclear
- Make changes promptly
- Confirm when complete

**Note:** Most contracts include 1-2 revision rounds. Additional major changes may require renegotiation.

### 4.6 Exercise: Delivery Simulation

1. Imagine you completed a logo design
2. Write a delivery message
3. List 3 files you would include
4. Practice responding to a revision request

## Module 5: Getting Paid (15 min)

### 5.1 How Payment Works

```
Client accepts proposal
        ↓
Client funds escrow (money held safely)
        ↓
You complete and submit work
        ↓
Client reviews and approves
        ↓
Payment released to your wallet
        ↓
Withdraw to your bank
```

### 5.2 Payment Timeline

| Event | When |
|-------|------|
| Escrow funded | After acceptance |
| Payment release | After client approval |
| In wallet | Instant after release |
| To bank account | Instant - 24 hours |

### 5.3 Platform Fee

```
Client pays: ₦100,000
Platform fee: ₦10,000 (10%)
You receive: ₦90,000
```

### 5.4 Withdrawing Your Earnings

1. Go to Wallet
2. Tap "Withdraw"
3. Select bank account
4. Enter amount
5. Enter PIN
6. Confirm

### 5.5 What if Client Doesn't Approve?

If there's a dispute:
1. Try to resolve with client via messages
2. If no resolution, contact support
3. HustleX reviews the case
4. Fair decision made based on evidence

## Module 6: Building Your Reputation (15 min)

### 6.1 Why Ratings Matter

| Rating | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| 5 stars | Top of search results, more invites |
| 4 stars | Good visibility |
| 3 stars | Reduced visibility |
| Below 3 | May affect account standing |

### 6.2 Getting Great Reviews

**Keys to 5-Star Reviews:**
- Deliver quality work
- Meet or beat deadlines
- Communicate professionally
- Be responsive
- Go slightly above expectations

### 6.3 Your Credit Score

Completing gigs improves your credit score:
- Each completed gig adds points
- High ratings boost your score more
- Consistent work builds credit history

**Benefits of Higher Credit Score:**
- Access to loans
- Higher transaction limits
- Trust badge on profile

### 6.4 Long-term Success Tips

1. **Specialize** - Become known for specific skills
2. **Be Consistent** - Maintain quality standards
3. **Build Relationships** - Repeat clients are gold
4. **Keep Learning** - Update skills regularly
5. **Stay Active** - Regular activity helps visibility
