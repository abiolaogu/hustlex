### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**LTV Calculation (3-Year Cohort):**

| Component | Year 1 Cohort | Year 3 Cohort | Year 5 Cohort |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|---------------|
| Year 1 ARPU | ₦333 | ₦1,333 | ₦3,000 |
| Year 2 ARPU (60% retain) | ₦800 × 0.6 = ₦480 | ₦2,000 × 0.7 = ₦1,400 | ₦4,000 × 0.8 = ₦3,200 |
| Year 3 ARPU (40% retain) | ₦1,500 × 0.4 = ₦600 | ₦3,000 × 0.5 = ₦1,500 | ₦5,000 × 0.6 = ₦3,000 |
| **3-Year LTV** | **₦1,413** | **₦4,233** | **₦9,200** |
| Gross Margin Adjusted | ₦763 | ₦2,751 | ₦7,084 |
