# 08. Go-To-Market Strategy

---

## GTM Overview

HustleX's go-to-market strategy focuses on **community-led growth** in a concentrated geographic market before expanding. We will dominate Lagos's gig worker segment before scaling nationally.

---

## Target Segment Prioritization

### Segment Priority Matrix

| Segment | Pain Intensity | Accessibility | Virality | LTV Potential | **Priority** |
|---------|---------------|---------------|----------|---------------|--------------|
| Ride-hailing drivers | 5 | 5 | 4 | 4 | **#1** |
| Delivery riders | 5 | 4 | 4 | 3 | **#2** |
| Artisans (electricians, plumbers) | 5 | 3 | 5 | 5 | **#3** |
| Freelancers (designers, devs) | 4 | 5 | 4 | 5 | **#4** |
| Market traders | 4 | 3 | 5 | 4 | **#5** |
| Beauty professionals | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | **#6** |

*(Scale: 1-5, higher is better)*

### Phase 1: Lagos Gig Workers (Month 1-6)

**Primary Target:** Ride-hailing drivers (Bolt, Uber, InDriver)
- Size: 150,000+ in Lagos
- Concentration: Driver hubs, fueling stations
- Pain: Weekly pay from platforms, need daily cash
- Entry: Transport union partnerships

**Secondary Target:** Delivery riders
- Size: 50,000+ in Lagos
- Concentration: Restaurant clusters, dispatch hubs
- Pain: Inconsistent gig availability
- Entry: Logistics company partnerships

### Phase 2: Expand Verticals (Month 7-12)

**Add Targets:**
- Artisans (via trade associations)
- Freelancers (via tech communities)
- Beauty professionals (via salon networks)

### Phase 3: Geographic Expansion (Month 13-24)

**Cities in Order:**
1. Abuja (Month 13-15)
2. Port Harcourt (Month 16-18)
3. Ibadan, Kano (Month 19-24)

### Phase 4: Regional Expansion (Month 25-36)

**Markets:**
- Ghana (similar dynamics, English-speaking)
- Diaspora corridors (UK, US)

## Sales Process

### B2C: Self-Serve Funnel

```
AWARENESS          CONSIDERATION       CONVERSION         ACTIVATION
    │                   │                  │                  │
    ▼                   ▼                  ▼                  ▼
┌─────────┐       ┌─────────┐        ┌─────────┐       ┌─────────┐
│ Social  │       │ App     │        │Download │       │ First   │
│ Media   │──────▶│ Store   │───────▶│ + Sign  │──────▶│ Trans-  │
│ Ad/Post │       │ Page    │        │ Up      │       │ action  │
└─────────┘       └─────────┘        └─────────┘       └─────────┘
     │                 │                  │                  │
   1M impressions    100K visits       30K downloads     15K activated
     │                 │                  │                  │
   10% CTR           30% conversion    50% activation    Target
```

**Conversion Targets:**
- Impression → Click: 10%
- Click → Download: 30%
- Download → Signup: 80%
- Signup → Activated: 50%

### B2B: Partnership Sales

**Sales Process:**

| Stage | Activities | Duration | Conversion |
|-------|------------|----------|------------|
| **Identify** | Research associations, unions, employers | Ongoing | - |
| **Outreach** | Cold outreach, warm intros | 1-2 weeks | 20% respond |
| **Demo** | Product demo, value proposition | 1 meeting | 50% interested |
| **Pilot** | Small-scale trial (100-500 users) | 4-8 weeks | 60% success |
| **Contract** | Terms negotiation, agreement | 2-4 weeks | 80% close |
| **Onboard** | Integration, training | 2-4 weeks | 95% launch |
| **Expand** | Grow to full membership | Ongoing | 50% penetration |

**Target Accounts (Year 1):**

### Metric Definitions

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| **Acquisition** | App download | 100K/month by Month 12 |
| **Activation** | Complete KYC + first transaction | 60% of downloads |
| **Retention** | Active in Month 2+ | 50% M1 retention |
| **Revenue** | Makes revenue-generating action | 20% of MAU |
| **Referral** | Sends at least 1 successful referral | 30% of active users |

### Activation Checklist

User is "activated" when they complete:
- [ ] Download app
- [ ] Phone verification (OTP)
- [ ] Basic KYC (name, photo)
- [ ] Set transaction PIN
- [ ] First transaction (deposit, transfer, or gig action)

**Activation UX Focus:**
- Reduce onboarding to <3 minutes
- Progressive profiling (don't ask everything upfront)
- Quick win: first P2P transfer or bill payment
- Gamification: progress bar, rewards

## Growth Experiments (Year 1)

### Experiment Backlog

| Experiment | Hypothesis | Metric | Duration |
|------------|------------|--------|----------|
| Referral bonus increase | Higher bonus = more referrals | Referral rate | 2 weeks |
| Agent gamification | Leaderboard increases signups | Agent productivity | 4 weeks |
| Savings challenge | Challenges increase engagement | Retention | 4 weeks |
| Early access credit | Credit offer increases activation | Activation rate | 2 weeks |

| Offline-first mode | Works without data = more usage | Session length | 4 weeks |
