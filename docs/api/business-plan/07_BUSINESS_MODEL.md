# 07. Business Model

---

## Business Model Overview

HustleX operates a **platform business model** with multiple integrated revenue streams. Our model creates value by connecting gig workers with clients, facilitating community savings, and providing credit based on platform activity.

---

### Revenue Stream Details

#### 1. Gig Transaction Fees (16% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- Client posts gig and funds escrow
- HustleX holds funds until work is approved
- On release, 1.5% fee deducted from payment

**Pricing:**
| Transaction Size | Fee Rate | Example |
|------------------|----------|---------|
| ₦1,000 - ₦50,000 | 1.5% | ₦10,000 gig = ₦150 fee |
| ₦50,001 - ₦500,000 | 1.5% | ₦100,000 gig = ₦1,500 fee |
| ₦500,001+ | 1.25% | ₦1,000,000 gig = ₦12,500 fee |

**Projections:**
| Year | Gig GMV | Take Rate | Revenue |
|------|---------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | ₦1B | 1.5% | ₦15M |
| 3 | ₦20B | 1.5% | ₦300M |
| 5 | ₦160B | 1.5% | ₦2.4B |

#### 2. Lending Interest Income (40% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- Users build HustleScore through platform activity
- Eligible users can apply for micro-loans
- Interest charged on outstanding principal

**Pricing:**
| Loan Product | Amount | Term | Monthly Rate | APR Equivalent |
|--------------|--------|------|--------------|----------------|
| Credit Builder | ₦5-20K | 7-14 days | 5% | 60% |
| Emergency | ₦10-50K | 7-30 days | 4.5% | 54% |
| Standard | ₦50-200K | 30-90 days | 4% | 48% |
| Premium | ₦200-500K | 60-180 days | 3% | 36% |

*Note: Rates are competitive with Carbon (5%+) and significantly below loan sharks (15-25% monthly)*

**Projections:**
| Year | Loan Book | Avg Rate | Gross Interest | Net (after defaults) |
|------|-----------|----------|----------------|---------------------|
| 1 | ₦200M | 4% | ₦16M | ₦10M |
| 3 | ₦10B | 3.8% | ₦912M | ₦800M |
| 5 | ₦80B | 3.5% | ₦6.7B | ₦6B |

*Assumes 5-8% default rate depending on year*

#### 3. Savings Circle Fees (4% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- Circles pay a small fee per contribution cycle
- Fee covers platform maintenance, dispute resolution, optional insurance

**Pricing:**
| Circle Contribution | Fee per Cycle | Fee Type |
|--------------------|---------------|----------|
| ₦1,000 - ₦10,000 | ₦100 | Per member per cycle |
| ₦10,001 - ₦50,000 | ₦200 | Per member per cycle |
| ₦50,001 - ₦100,000 | ₦300 | Per member per cycle |
| ₦100,001+ | ₦500 | Per member per cycle |

**Optional Insurance:** 1% of circle pool (protects against member default)

**Projections:**
| Year | Active Circles | Avg Members | Avg Fee | Revenue |
|------|----------------|-------------|---------|---------|
| 1 | 2,000 | 10 | ₦100 | ₦2M |
| 3 | 50,000 | 12 | ₦167 | ₦100M |
| 5 | 200,000 | 15 | ₦200 | ₦600M |

#### 4. Bill Payment Commissions (6% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- Users pay bills (airtime, data, utilities) through wallet
- HustleX earns commission from billers

**Pricing:**
| Bill Type | Commission | Example |

| Airtime | 2.5% | ₦1,000 = ₦25 commission |
| Data | 2% | ₦2,000 = ₦40 commission |
| Electricity | 1.5% | ₦10,000 = ₦150 commission |
| Cable TV | 2% | ₦5,000 = ₦100 commission |

**Projections:**

| 3 | ₦7.5B | 2% | ₦150M |
| 5 | ₦45B | 2% | ₦900M |

#### 5. Premium Subscriptions (10% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- Tiered subscription plans with premium features
- Freemium model converts engaged users

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | ₦0 | Basic wallet, join circles, browse gigs, credit builder |
| **Hustle+** | ₦500/month | Create circles (5), priority support, lower loan rates (-0.5%) |
| **Hustle Pro** | ₦2,000/month | Create circles (unlimited), business tools, API access, premium credit, dedicated support |
| **Business** | ₦10,000/month | Team features, bulk payouts, analytics, custom integrations |

**Projections:**
| Year | Premium Users | ARPU | Revenue |
|------|---------------|------|---------|
| 1 | 5,000 | ₦1,000 | ₦5M |
| 3 | 100,000 | ₦2,500 | ₦250M |
| 5 | 500,000 | ₦3,000 | ₦1.5B |

#### 6. Float Income (8% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- User wallet balances held in treasury instruments
- HustleX earns return on aggregate float

**Assumptions:**
- Average wallet balance: ₦5,000-15,000 per active user
- Treasury return: 12-15% annually (T-bills, money market)
- HustleX keeps 100% of float income

**Projections:**
| Year | Active Users | Avg Balance | Total Float | Return | Revenue |
|------|--------------|-------------|-------------|--------|---------|
| 1 | 100K | ₦5,000 | ₦500M | 10% | ₦5M |
| 3 | 1M | ₦10,000 | ₦10B | 15% | ₦200M |
| 5 | 4M | ₦15,000 | ₦60B | 15% | ₦1.2B |

#### 7. Data & API Revenue (16% of Y5 Revenue)

**How it works:**
- B2B data products (anonymized, aggregated insights)
- API access for third-party developers
- White-label partnerships

**Products:**
| Product | Price | Target Customer |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Credit Data API | ₦50-200 per query | Lenders, banks |
| Workforce Insights | ₦500K-5M/year | Corporates, HR |
| White-label Ajo | Revenue share | Banks, cooperatives |
| Payment API | 0.5% of volume | Merchants, platforms |

**Projections:**
| Year | Revenue | Primary Sources |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| 1 | ₦10M | Early API pilots |
| 3 | ₦200M | Credit data + API |
| 5 | ₦2.4B | Full B2B suite |

### Pricing Principles

1. **Free to start** - No barrier to entry
2. **Value-based upgrades** - Pay for features that add clear value
3. **Competitive with alternatives** - Below loan sharks, comparable to digital lenders
4. **Transparent** - No hidden fees, clear pricing
5. **Inclusive** - Lowest tiers accessible to lowest incomes

## Unit Economics

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 3 | Year 5 | Target |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| **CAC** | ₦1,500 | ₦1,000 | ₦800 | <₦1,000 |
| **LTV** | ₦15,000 | ₦30,000 | ₦45,000 | >₦30,000 |
| **LTV/CAC** | 10x | 30x | 56x | >10x |
| **Gross Margin** | 55% | 65% | 70% | >60% |
| **Contribution Margin** | 25% | 45% | 55% | >40% |
| **Payback Period** | 6 months | 4 months | 3 months | <6 months |
| **Monthly Churn** | 5% | 3% | 2% | <5% |

### CAC Breakdown

| Channel | Year 1 CAC | Mix | Blended |
|---------|------------|-----|---------|
| Organic/referral | ₦500 | 30% | ₦150 |
| Digital marketing | ₦2,000 | 40% | ₦800 |
| Agent/BTL | ₦2,500 | 20% | ₦500 |
| Partnerships | ₦1,000 | 10% | ₦100 |
| **Blended CAC** | | | **₦1,550** |

### LTV Calculation

```
LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin) / Monthly Churn Rate

Year 1:
- ARPU: ₦500/month (blended across users)
- Gross Margin: 55%
- Churn: 5%/month
- LTV = (₦500 × 0.55) / 0.05 = ₦5,500

Wait, let's use 3-year cohort approach:

Year 1 LTV (3-year horizon):
- Year 1 ARPU: ₦500 × 12 = ₦6,000
- Year 2 ARPU: ₦800 × 12 = ₦9,600 (50% retain)
- Year 3 ARPU: ₦1,200 × 12 = ₦14,400 (25% retain)
- Total: ₦6,000 + (₦9,600 × 0.5) + (₦14,400 × 0.25) = ₦14,400

LTV Year 1 ≈ ₦15,000 (rounded)
```

## Summary

HustleX's business model is designed for:

1. **Multiple revenue streams** - Reduces dependence on any single source
2. **Network effects** - More users improve platform for everyone
3. **Data monetization** - Platform activity becomes valuable asset
4. **Scalable unit economics** - LTV/CAC improves with scale
5. **Path to profitability** - Clear line of sight to positive EBITDA
