## Year 1 OKRs

### Q1 OKRs (Phase 0: Foundation)

**Objective 1: Complete production-ready product**
- KR1: Test coverage reaches 70%
- KR2: Zero critical security vulnerabilities
- KR3: App approved in iOS and Google Play stores

**Objective 2: Secure seed funding**
- KR1: Pitch to 25+ investors
- KR2: Close $500K seed round
- KR3: Assemble 3 strategic advisors

**Objective 3: Establish regulatory foundation**
- KR1: CBN sandbox application submitted
- KR2: NDPR registration complete
- KR3: Legal structure finalized

### Q2 OKRs (Phase 1: Launch)

**Objective 1: Achieve successful public launch**
- KR1: 25,000 registered users
- KR2: 15,000 activated users (completed KYC + transaction)
- KR3: App store rating >4.0

**Objective 2: Validate product-market fit**
- KR1: 40% Month 1 retention rate
- KR2: NPS score >30
- KR3: ₦50M monthly GMV

**Objective 3: Build operational foundation**
- KR1: 50 agents recruited and active
- KR2: Customer support response time <2 hours
- KR3: Fraud loss rate <0.5% of GMV

### Q3 OKRs (Phase 2: Growth)

**Objective 1: Scale user acquisition**
- KR1: 75,000 registered users
- KR2: CAC below ₦1,500
- KR3: 30% of new users from referrals

**Objective 2: Grow financial products**
- KR1: ₦150M loans disbursed (cumulative)
- KR2: Default rate below 8%
- KR3: 1,000 active savings circles

**Objective 3: Expand geographic footprint**
- KR1: Abuja launch with 5,000 users
- KR2: 100 agents across Lagos and Abuja
- KR3: Port Harcourt launch preparation complete

### Q4 OKRs (Phase 2: Growth continued)

**Objective 1: Achieve annual targets**
- KR1: 150,000 registered users
- KR2: 100,000 monthly active users
- KR3: ₦1B monthly GMV

**Objective 2: Demonstrate unit economics**
- KR1: LTV/CAC ratio >10x
- KR2: Gross margin >55%
- KR3: Monthly burn <$50K

**Objective 3: Prepare for Series A**
- KR1: Financial model updated with actuals
- KR2: Data room prepared
- KR3: Identified 10+ Series A prospects
