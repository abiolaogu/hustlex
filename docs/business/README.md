## Document Summary

### Business Plan
The complete business plan covering:
- Executive summary and company overview
- Products and services breakdown
- Market analysis (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- Competitive analysis
- Marketing strategy
- Operations plan
- Financial projections
- Team and milestones

### Business Model Canvas
Visual representation of:
- Customer segments
- Value propositions
- Channels and relationships
- Revenue streams
- Key resources, activities, and partners
- Cost structure

### Go-to-Market Strategy
Detailed launch and growth plan:
- Geographic phasing (Lagos → National)
- Customer acquisition channels
- Referral and viral mechanics
- Messaging and campaigns
- Partnership strategy
- Success metrics

### Financial Projections
5-year financial model including:
- Revenue by stream
- Cost structure
- Cash flow projections
- Unit economics (LTV/CAC)
- Funding requirements
- Valuation scenarios

### Competitive Analysis
Strategic assessment covering:
- Market landscape mapping
- Direct competitor profiles
- Feature comparison matrix
- Competitive positioning
- Win/loss analysis
- Battlecards

### Risk Assessment
Comprehensive risk management:
- Risk matrix and scoring
- Strategic, operational, financial risks
- Mitigation strategies
- Crisis management plan
- Risk monitoring framework

### KPI & Metrics Framework
Performance measurement system:
- North Star metric (WATU)
- Growth, engagement, revenue metrics
- Product-specific KPIs
- Unit economics tracking
- Dashboard and reporting structure
