### 4.2 Customer Lifetime Value

**LTV Calculation:**

```
LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin × Avg Lifespan) - CAC

Year 3 Example:
- Monthly ARPU: ₦400
- Gross Margin: 73%
- Avg Lifespan: 48 months
- LTV = (₦400 × 73% × 48) = ₦14,016
- CAC = ₦800
- LTV/CAC = 17.5x
```
