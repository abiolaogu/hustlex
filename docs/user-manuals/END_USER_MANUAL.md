## 4. Savings Circles (Ajo/Esusu)

### 4.1 What is Ajo/Esusu?

Ajo (or Esusu) is a traditional Nigerian savings method where a group contributes money regularly, and members take turns receiving the total pool.

**Example:**
- 10 members contribute ‚Ç¶10,000 weekly
- Each week, one member receives ‚Ç¶100,000
- After 10 weeks, everyone has received once

### 4.2 Types of Savings Circles

**Rotational (Traditional Ajo):**
- Members take turns receiving the pool
- Position determines when you receive
- Perfect for planned expenses

**Fixed Target:**
- Everyone saves toward a goal
- No rotation - save at your own pace
- Good for group savings goals

### 4.3 Joining a Circle

1. Tap **"Savings"** tab
2. Browse available circles or enter invite code
3. Review circle details:
   - Contribution amount
   - Frequency (weekly/monthly)
   - Number of members
   - Your assigned position
4. Tap **"Join Circle"**

### 4.4 Creating Your Own Circle

1. Tap **"Savings"** ‚Üí **"Create Circle"**
2. Set up your circle:
   - **Name:** Give it a memorable name
   - **Type:** Rotational or Fixed Target
   - **Contribution:** Amount per period
   - **Frequency:** Weekly or Monthly
   - **Max Members:** How many can join
   - **Start Date:** When contributions begin
3. Share the **invite code** with friends

### 4.5 Making Contributions

**Automatic Contributions:**
1. Enable auto-pay in circle settings
2. Ensure wallet has sufficient balance
3. Contributions deducted automatically on due date

**Manual Contributions:**
1. Open your circle
2. Tap **"Contribute Now"**
3. Confirm the amount
4. Enter your PIN

> **Important:** Late or missed contributions affect your credit score!

### 4.6 Receiving Your Payout

When it's your turn (rotational) or target is reached (fixed):
1. You'll receive a notification
2. Funds are automatically added to your wallet
3. View payout in transaction history

### 5.1 Understanding Your Credit Score

Your HustleX credit score ranges from 300-850:

| Score Range | Rating | Loan Eligibility |
|-------------|--------|------------------|
| 750-850 | Excellent | Up to ‚Ç¶500,000 |
| 700-749 | Good | Up to ‚Ç¶200,000 |
| 650-699 | Fair | Up to ‚Ç¶100,000 |
| 550-649 | Poor | Up to ‚Ç¶50,000 |
| 300-549 | Very Poor | Not eligible |

### 5.2 How Your Score is Calculated

Your score is based on:

| Factor | Weight | How to Improve |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Payment History | 30% | Pay on time, every time |
| Savings Consistency | 25% | Contribute regularly |
| Gig Performance | 25% | Complete gigs successfully |
| Account Age | 10% | Keep using HustleX |
| Platform Activity | 10% | Stay active on the app |

### 5.3 Checking Your Credit Score

1. Tap **"Credit"** tab
2. View your current score
3. See breakdown of factors
4. Get tips to improve

### 5.4 Applying for a Loan

1. Tap **"Credit"** ‚Üí **"Apply for Loan"**
2. Select loan amount (within your limit)
3. Choose tenure (1-6 months)
4. Review terms:
   - Interest rate (5% monthly)
   - Total repayment amount
   - Monthly payment
5. Accept terms and apply
6. Funds disbursed instantly to wallet

### 5.5 Repaying Your Loan

**Option 1: Auto-debit**
- Set up automatic monthly payments
- Never miss a payment
- Improves credit score

**Option 2: Manual Payment**
1. Go to **"Credit"** ‚Üí **"My Loans"**
2. Select active loan
3. Tap **"Make Payment"**
4. Enter amount (can pay extra)
5. Enter PIN and confirm

> **Early repayment bonus:** Pay off early with no penalty and boost your credit score!

### 7.1 Protecting Your Account

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Keep your PIN secret
- Enable biometric login
- Log out on shared devices
- Verify BVN/NIN for higher limits
- Report suspicious activity immediately

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Share your OTP with anyone

- Click links in suspicious messages

### 7.2 Recognizing Scams

**HustleX will NEVER:**
- Ask for your PIN via call/message
- Request OTP for "account upgrade"
- Ask you to transfer money to "unlock" funds
- Send links to login outside the app

**If something seems wrong:**
1. Don't click suspicious links
2. Don't share any codes
3. Report to support@hustlex.app
4. Change your PIN immediately

## 8. FAQs

### Account & Login

**Q: I'm not receiving OTP, what should I do?**
A:
1. Check if your phone has network
2. Wait 60 seconds and request again
3. Check your spam/blocked messages
4. Contact support if issue persists

**Q: I forgot my PIN, how do I reset it?**
A:
1. Tap "Forgot PIN" on PIN entry screen
2. Verify with OTP sent to your phone
3. Set a new PIN

### Wallet

**Q: Why is my withdrawal delayed?**
A: Withdrawals are usually instant but may take up to 24 hours during bank maintenance or high traffic.

**Q: What are the deposit limits?**
A: No maximum deposit limit. Minimum deposit is ‚Ç¶100.

### Gigs

**Q: How long until I get paid for a gig?**
A: Payment is released immediately after the client approves your work.

**Q: What if the client doesn't approve my work?**
A: If there's a dispute, contact support. We'll review and mediate fairly.

### Savings

**Q: What happens if I miss a contribution?**
A:
- You'll receive reminders before due date
- Late contributions may incur penalties
- Repeated misses may affect credit score
- You may be removed from circle after 2 misses

**Q: Can I leave a circle early?**
A: You can request to leave, but may forfeit contributions if you haven't received payout yet.

### Credit & Loans

**Q: Why was my loan application rejected?**
A: Common reasons:
- Credit score too low
- Existing unpaid loan
- Account too new
- Insufficient activity

**Q: What happens if I miss a loan payment?**
A:
- 3-day grace period
- Late fee after grace period
- Credit score decreases
- May affect future loan eligibility
